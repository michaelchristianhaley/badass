\# control/wins/README.md



This directory records specific, evidence-backed instances where BADASS

recovery or discipline produced a verifiably better outcome than the

assistant's default behavior would have. It is not a praise log.



A record here requires:

\- what the assistant was about to do, or had done, before correction;

\- the specific BADASS section that changed the outcome;

\- what The User observed as different or better, stated in their own words;

\- why that outcome couldn't have been confirmed without direct evidence

&#x20; (i.e., it isn't just "The User said it went well").



A single confirmed instance is sufficient to record here. Promotion to

`WISDOM.md` still requires the assistant or The User to judge the lesson

generalizable beyond the specific instance, per the existing promotion

rule — recording a win here is not itself a promotion.



The Assistant may propose an entry when The User points out a specific

good outcome. The Assistant shall not log its own performance as a win

unprompted, and shall not treat accumulated wins as evidence toward any

compliance-matrix `PASS` — those still require the direct evidence that

section already demands.
